Problem Statement Alignment
Understand — The onboarding questionnaire establishes a personalized baseline, and the Insights dashboard breaks down your emissions by category with visual charts and relatable equivalents.

Track — The Activity Logger lets you log daily transport, energy, food, and consumption habits with minimal friction, including recurring habits. All data persists in localStorage.

Reduce — The Recommendations Engine generates specific, ranked suggestions based on your actual logged data — not generic tips. You can commit to recommendations and track follow-through.

Personalized — Every insight, recommendation, and equivalent is calculated from your own data. Your biggest contributor and best quick win are unique to your habits.

Achievable — Goals & gamification with streaks, badges, and milestones encourage sustained behavior change. Bite-sized education builds genuine understanding without guilt.
